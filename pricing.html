Common questions
Is DentScan a replacement for a dentist?
No. DentScan provides general observations based on your photo and is not a medical diagnostic tool. Always consult a licensed dentist for professional advice.
How accurate is the AI analysis?
DentScan uses advanced AI vision to detect visible signs like discoloration, plaque, and gum redness. Accuracy depends on photo quality and lighting.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel at any time from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Is my photo data safe?
Your photos are only used for analysis and are never stored on our servers. We take privacy seriously.
