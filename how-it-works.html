Quick guide
How DentScan works
Get insights about your dental health in 3 simple steps — it only takes a minute.
1
📸 Take a photo
Open your mouth wide and snap a clear photo of your teeth. Use good lighting — natural daylight works best. Our camera guide helps you position perfectly.
2
🔍 AI analysis
Our AI examines your photo for visible signs like plaque buildup, gum redness, discoloration, and other indicators. Analysis takes just a few seconds.
3
💡 Get your results
Receive a health score with detailed observations and personalized recommendations. If needed, we'll suggest scheduling a dentist visit.

Tips for best results
💡 Use bright, natural lighting — stand near a window or use your bathroom mirror light.
📱 Hold your phone steady, about 15-20cm from your mouth for the clearest image.
😁 Open wide! Show both upper and lower teeth for a complete analysis.
🪞 Use the front camera with our stencil guide, or ask someone to help with the back camera.
