Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

DentScan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our dental health analysis application.

Information We Collect

When you use DentScan, we may collect:

  • Photos you upload: Images of your teeth submitted for analysis. These are processed ephemerally and transmitted securely to our AI partner for analysis.
  • Usage data: Anonymous information about how you use the app, such as scan frequency and feature usage.
  • Device information: Browser type, device type, and operating system for optimization purposes.
  • Payment information: Processed securely through Lemon Squeezy. We do not store your payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered dental health analysis
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To process payments for premium features
  • To communicate with you about your account or service updates

Photo Data Handling

Your privacy is our priority. Here's exactly how your dental photos are processed:

  • Ephemeral processing: Photos are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on DentScan servers
  • AI partner transmission: Images are securely transmitted to Anthropic (our AI provider) via encrypted connection (TLS 1.3) for analysis
  • Anthropic's data handling: Anthropic processes images under their enterprise privacy standards and does not use your data to train AI models when accessed via API
  • No third-party sharing: Your images are not shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties
  • Local thumbnails only: Premium users' scan history stores small thumbnails locally in your browser (localStorage), not on our servers

Transparency note: While we do not store your photos on our servers, the image data does pass through Anthropic's systems for AI analysis. We recommend reviewing Anthropic's Privacy Policy for complete information on their data practices.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure payment processing through trusted third-party providers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide DentScan:

  • Anthropic Claude API: Powers our AI image analysis. Your dental photos are transmitted to Anthropic's servers for processing. Anthropic is bound by enterprise-grade privacy standards and their Privacy Policy.
  • Lemon Squeezy: Handles secure payment processing. We never see or store your full card details.
  • Vercel: Provides hosting and infrastructure with enterprise security standards.
  • OpenStreetMap: Powers the "Find a Dentist" map feature (no personal data shared).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

⚕️ Medical Disclaimer

NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE: DentScan is an educational and wellness screening tool only. It does not provide medical diagnoses, professional dental advice, or treatment recommendations.

Always seek the advice of a qualified dentist, physician, or other licensed healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding a dental or medical condition. Never disregard professional advice or delay seeking it because of information provided by DentScan. For our complete medical disclaimer, please visit our Medical Disclaimer page.
