Not Medical Advice

The information provided by DentScan is for general informational and educational purposes only. It is NOT intended to be, and should NOT be used as:

  • A substitute for professional dental examination
  • A medical diagnosis or treatment recommendation
  • A replacement for advice from a licensed dentist or healthcare provider

AI Limitations

DentScan uses artificial intelligence to analyze photos of teeth. Please be aware that:

  • AI analysis is based solely on visible features in photos
  • The AI cannot detect issues beneath the surface of teeth or gums
  • Photo quality, lighting, and angle significantly affect accuracy
  • The AI may miss conditions or provide inaccurate observations
  • No AI system can replace a professional clinical examination with proper dental tools and X-rays

Intended Use

DentScan is designed to:

  • Provide general observations about visible dental features
  • Encourage users to maintain good oral hygiene
  • Suggest when a professional dental visit may be beneficial
  • Serve as an educational tool about dental health

When to Seek Professional Care

You should always consult a dentist if you experience:

  • Tooth pain or sensitivity
  • Bleeding, swollen, or painful gums
  • Loose teeth
  • Persistent bad breath
  • Visible damage, chips, or cracks
  • Any concerning changes in your oral health

Do not delay seeking professional dental care based on DentScan results.
