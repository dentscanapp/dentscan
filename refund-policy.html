Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

✓ 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We want you to be completely satisfied with DentScan. If you're not happy, we'll refund your purchase within 14 days.

Single Scan Purchases ($2.99)

For one-time single scan purchases:

  • Before use: Full refund available within 14 days of purchase
  • After use: Refund available if the analysis failed due to a technical issue on our end
  • Photo quality issues: If your scan failed due to poor photo quality, we encourage you to try again with a clearer photo rather than requesting a refund

Premium Monthly Subscription ($7.99/month)

For monthly Premium subscriptions:

  • First 14 days: Full refund of your first month's payment if you're not satisfied
  • After 14 days: You may cancel anytime, but no refund for the current billing period
  • Cancellation: Your access continues until the end of your paid period

Premium Yearly Subscription ($49.99/year)

For yearly Premium subscriptions:

  • First 14 days: Full refund available, no questions asked
  • 14-30 days: Prorated refund available (minus any used months)
  • After 30 days: No refund, but you may cancel to prevent auto-renewal

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at info@dentscan.app
  • Include your purchase email address
  • Briefly explain the reason for your refund request
  • We typically process refunds within 3-5 business days

Refund Processing

All payments are processed through Lemon Squeezy. Refunds will be:

  • Returned to your original payment method
  • Processed within 5-10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • Issued in the original currency of purchase

Non-Refundable Situations

We cannot offer refunds in the following cases:

  • Requests made after the refund period has expired
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • Multiple refund requests (abuse of policy)
  • Dissatisfaction with AI analysis results (as stated in our Medical Disclaimer, DentScan provides general observations, not medical diagnoses)

Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Access continues until the end of your current billing period
  • No further charges after cancellation
  • You can re-subscribe at any time

Questions?

If you have questions about our refund policy or need assistance, please contact us at info@dentscan.app. We're here to help!
